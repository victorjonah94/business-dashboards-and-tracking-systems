# High-Ticket Funnel Strategy for Custom Business Tracking Systems (Abuja, Nigeria)

## 1) Positioning and Offer Refinement (Premium, Not Template-Based)

### Core Positioning Statement
I help Abuja-based business owners and CEOs replace guesswork with a custom Business Tracking & Control System (sales, expenses, inventory, dashboards) built in Google Sheets, implemented with their team, and tied to weekly decision-making.

### Premium Differentiators
- **Custom-built from your workflow**: no generic templates.
- **Implementation-led**: setup + team onboarding + reporting cadence.
- **Decision outcome focus**: reduce leakage, improve profit visibility, accelerate decisions.
- **Local business context**: built for Nigerian operational realities (cash sales, mixed channels, manual records).

### Ideal Client Profile (ICP)
Prioritize prospects with:
- 5+ staff and recurring monthly transactions.
- Revenue complexity (multiple products, channels, branches, or sales reps).
- Evidence of data confusion (conflicting numbers, delayed reports, no clean P&L visibility).
- Budget capacity and urgency to fix operational blind spots.

### Offer Packaging (Core Promise)
Within 14–30 days, you get a working custom system that shows what is happening in your business daily/weekly and what to do next.

### Signature Offer Name (Examples)
- **Business Control System (BCS) Implementation**
- **CEO Visibility Dashboard Buildout**
- **Sales–Expenses–Inventory Command System**

### Offer Components
1. Discovery and data audit
2. System architecture and KPI map
3. Custom build (Google Sheets + automations as needed)
4. Team onboarding and SOP handover
5. 30-day optimization support

## 2) Complete Funnel Strategy

## A. Ad Angle and Messaging

### Core Angles for Abuja Founders/CEOs
1. **Profit Leakage Angle**: “You may be making sales and still losing money.”
2. **Control Angle**: “If your numbers come late, your decisions are late.”
3. **Scale Angle**: “You cannot scale chaos; you can only scale systems.”
4. **Founder Relief Angle**: “Stop chasing staff for reports every evening.”

### Messaging Formula
- Pain (disorganized data, delayed numbers, hidden losses)
- Consequence (cashflow stress, bad decisions, stalled growth)
- Solution (custom tracking system + implementation)
- Proof (clarity, faster decisions, control)
- Action (book strategy call)

## B. Landing Page Structure
1. Hero (clear promise + CTA)
2. Problem amplification (cost of no system)
3. Solution explanation (how your implementation works)
4. Benefits/outcomes (business control, margin clarity, accountability)
5. Authority/proof (experience, case snapshots, process)
6. Qualification (who this is for/not for)
7. CTA + booking/lead form

## C. Lead Capture Strategy
Use a **2-step capture**:
- Step 1: Simple form (name, phone, business type, monthly revenue band)
- Step 2: Qualification questions + call booking

Offer lead magnet only if needed for ad efficiency:
- “Business Tracking Health Check for Abuja SMEs” (scorecard)

## D. Qualification Process
Use a short scorecard before calls:
- Monthly revenue band
- Number of transactions/month
- Current tracking method
- Team size
- Urgency (now, 1–3 months, later)
- Budget readiness (₦100k+)

Disqualify politely when:
- They only want a cheap template
- No real data volume yet
- No decision maker on call

## E. Sales Process (Call or Physical Meeting)
1. 10-min diagnosis (current process and leakage)
2. 10-min impact mapping (cost of current inefficiency)
3. 10-min solution walkthrough (custom roadmap)
4. 5-min offer and pricing options
5. 5-min close and next steps

For larger prospects, use physical meeting in Abuja after initial qualification call.

## F. Closing Strategy
- Present 2–3 options anchored to business outcomes.
- Use implementation slots scarcity (e.g., 3 new builds/month).
- Close with clear onboarding sequence:
  - Agreement + 60–70% deposit
  - Kickoff date
  - Data collection checklist

## 5) Consultation / Sales Call Script (Structured)

## A. Opening (2–3 mins)
“Thanks for your time. My goal is to understand how your business currently tracks performance, identify where money or visibility is leaking, and see whether we’re a fit to implement a proper control system.”

Set agenda:
1. Current tracking process
2. Gaps and impact
3. Recommended implementation plan
4. Next steps

## B. Diagnosis Questions (10–15 mins)
- How do you currently track sales daily/weekly?
- How are expenses recorded and reviewed?
- How do you track inventory movement and stock-outs?
- Which reports do you review weekly as CEO/founder?
- Where do inconsistencies usually happen?
- What decisions are delayed because data is unclear?
- What is this confusion currently costing you?

## C. Impact Quantification (5 mins)
Summarize:
“From what you shared, the biggest issues are [X], [Y], [Z]. This likely causes [delayed decisions / loss / weak accountability]. If unchanged for the next 6 months, the cost is significant in cashflow and growth.”

## D. Expert Positioning (3 mins)
“I don’t sell generic spreadsheets. I implement custom business tracking systems tied to your operation and team workflow, so you can see key numbers quickly and act with confidence.”

## E. Offer Transition (5 mins)
“Based on your situation, I recommend the [tier name] implementation. It includes audit, custom build, dashboard setup, onboarding, and optimization support. Timeline is [X days].”

## F. Pricing + Close (5 mins)
Give options and anchor value:
- Starter Implementation: ₦150k
- Growth Implementation: ₦300k
- Command Implementation: ₦500k+

Close:
“If you’re ready, we can secure your implementation slot today with [deposit amount], and begin kickoff on [date].”

## G. Objection Handling Framework

### “It’s expensive.”
“Understood. Compared to the cost of wrong decisions, missed leakage, and poor tracking each month, this pays for itself by improving control and profitability.”

### “Let me think about it.”
“Fair. What specifically would you like to think through — budget, timing, or fit? Let’s address that directly so you can decide with clarity.”

### “Can’t you just send a template?”
“I don’t offer one-size-fits-all templates because they fail in unique business operations. My service is custom implementation to ensure adoption and results.”

## 6) Pricing Strategy and Service Tiers (Nigeria Market Fit)

## Positioning Rule
Price by **business outcome and implementation depth**, not spreadsheet tabs.

## Suggested Tiers

### Tier 1: Starter Control Setup (₦100k – ₦180k)
Best for early structured SMEs.
- Basic sales + expense tracking
- Simple dashboard
- 1 onboarding session
- 2 weeks support

### Tier 2: Growth Control System (₦250k – ₦400k)
Best for established SMEs with team complexity.
- Sales + expenses + inventory integration
- Role-based data entry workflow
- KPI dashboard + weekly review structure
- 2–3 onboarding sessions
- 30 days support

### Tier 3: Command System Implementation (₦500k – ₦900k+)
Best for multi-unit or higher transaction businesses.
- Full process mapping
- Multi-branch / multi-team dashboard logic
- Advanced controls and audit checks
- Leadership reporting framework
- 45–60 days support and optimization

## Commercial Terms
- 60–70% upfront deposit
- Balance at delivery milestone
- Optional monthly retainer for ongoing reporting optimization (₦80k – ₦250k/month)
